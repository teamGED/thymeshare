How It Works

Our Purpose

ThymeShare was concieved over a backyard haul of jalepenos too plentiful to feed a nation. ThymeShare is an application that allows for the opportunity to share the food we grow with the community - expanding the access to and the menaing of local food. If we encourage and incentivize people to grow food we can have an entirely different (edible) urban landscape. One where you get to pick fruit and veg off the plant - not a shelf! And where your fresh food comes from a micro-farm, window garden, or fruit tree in your neighbour’s backyards.

Share Your Bounty

We allow anyone to post food that they grow themselves, or post produce growing in public space. Find Local Food

Are you hungry? Do your neighbors have a salad growing in their backyard but not enough bellies to feed? With ThymeShare you can easily search local produce based on proximty to where you are or what you need. Once you've found that uber-local produce that ignites your belly-buds you can contact the grower and coordinate a pickup. There is no required payment here because we believe that uneaten food is always better enjoyed than wasted however we encourage you to make a donation and show your appreciation to your grower!
